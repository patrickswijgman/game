## What are some core mechanics of these games?

- Gothic II
  - start as a noob, weapons and armor upgrades have high impact on stats (difficulty)
  - choose a specific class pathway
    - knight
    - archer
    - mage

- Dark Souls
  - iconic boss battles
  - bonfires that reset the level, heals and replenishes potions
  - weapon upgrades

- Book of Demons
  - deck builder that creates a character build

## What do I like about these games?

- Gothic II
  - simple stats
    - strength, dexterity, mana + magic circle
  - simple damage calculation
    - e.g. weapon damage + strength - protection
  - open world to explore
    - although you can end up in a higher level area
      - distance from the road?

- Dark Souls
  - combat
  - estus
  - build however you want via stat investment

- Book of Demons
  - you equip the cards to create a build
    - some cards reduce your maximum mana (cost)
    - some cards are unique (balancing)
